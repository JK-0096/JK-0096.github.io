About Joint Komodo

Joint Komodo began with someone who was hyperfixated on rare creatures, such as komodos, and learned more about them by watching them every day. From that, it evolved into a company that brings awareness to exotic animals, with knowledge to enthusiasts.

We provide facts and experiences. From feeding stations and interactive shows to kits for raising your own exotic pets to care for, Joint Komodo offers opportunities to form connections with creatures you may never have thought you would meet in real life.
